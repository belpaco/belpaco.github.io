  • 1980 - Ascended to the ranks of management, his leadership skills earning him the admiration of colleagues and competitors alike. (32)
  • 1985 - Started freelancing as a software developer, working remotely from his favorite coffee shop
  • 1985 - Captured the heart of his soulmate, embarking on a journey of love and companionship that would span a lifetime.
